We Are The Premier University In Ghana

Computer science is the foundation of the information technology era that we are living in. Therefore, if society is to achieve quick economic advancements and fully benefit from information technology, computer science education is essential. The study and creation of programs that enable us to use computers on a daily basis, the software systems that underpin these applications, and the techniques and procedures employed to create applications and systems that function dependably, accurately, and morally are all included in computer science. The core of computer science is computer programming, and companies hiring computer scientists look for skilled programmers who understand the latest methods and technologies and can apply good engineering concepts to the creation, building, and upkeep of computer programs.

Whether we are introducing youth to an endless future or using fearless research to take on the great concerns of our day.
